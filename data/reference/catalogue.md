## Things we do not do

These come up regularly. We don't offer them — refer or decline politely:

- Roofing, gutter cleaning, or anything requiring full roof access.
- Solar panel installation or repair (refer: SunPath Energy).
- Pool plumbing or pool equipment (refer: AquaCorp Pools).
- Appliance repair (washing machines, dishwashers, ovens) — we install but do not repair.
- Anything in commercial premises larger than 200m². We are residential-only.
- Locksmithing, glazing, pest control.

## Service area

We service residential addresses within 40km of the Sydney CBD. Anything outside this radius should be flagged for human review — Sales will assess case-by-case. Customers in our service area but on a strata property should be told that body corporate approval is their responsibility, not ours.

## Payment & billing notes

- Standard terms: payment due within 14 days of invoice.
- Jobs over $2,000 require 30% deposit before work commences.
- We accept card, bank transfer, and PayID. We no longer accept cheques (since Jan 2024).
- Refunds for completed work are handled on a case-by-case basis through Customer Care.
